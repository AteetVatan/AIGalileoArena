# Proposal 29: Neon Samurai (Cyber-Feudalism)

## Philosophy
"Neon Samurai" interprets the debate as a **Duel of Honor** in a Neo-Tokyo setting. The robots are Samurai Androids.

## Design Tokens

### Aesthetic
- **Theme**: Ghost in the Shell / Cyberpunk 2077 (Arasaka style).
- **Visuals**: Rain, Neon Kanji, Katanas.

### Colors
- **Palette**: Midnight Blue, Neon Pink, White.
- **Accents**: Cherry Blossoms (Holographic).

### Typography
- **Font**: `Noto Sans JP` + `Orbitron`.
- **Style**: Vertical text layouts mixed with horizontal data.

## Key Components

### 1. The Duelists
- **Orthodox (Ronin)**: Blue energy blade.
- **Heretic (Ninja)**: Red energy daggers.

### 2. The Strike (Debate)
When a message sends, the avatar "slashes" across the screen. The text appears in the "wound" (a split graphic).

### 3. The Shogun (Judge)
A masked figure in the background, sitting on a throne of servers.

## UX Flow
1.  **Atmosphere**: Animated rain falls in the background (CSS).
2.  **Intensity**: Glitch effects on the text when arguments rely on weak evidence (hallucinations).
