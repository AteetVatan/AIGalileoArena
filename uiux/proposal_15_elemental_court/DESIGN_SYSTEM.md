# Design System: Elemental Court (Proposal 15)

## Philosophy
"Elemental Court" posits that AI debate is akin to a natural phenomenonâ€”a storm of conflicting forces. The visual language is raw, chaotic, and beautiful, using fire, ice, and lightning metaphors.

## Color Palette
- **Abyss Black**: `#000000`
- **Plasma Fire**: `#FF4500`
- **Cryo Ice**: `#00BFFF`
- **Lightning Violet**: `#8A2BE2`
- **Core White**: `#FFFFFF`

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Cinzel', serif (Epic, mythological)
- **Body**: 'Raleway', sans-serif (Elegant, thin)

## Components
- **Elemental Swirls**: Particle systems representing different arguments (Fire = Passion/Aggression, Ice = Logic/Cold).
- **The Singularity (Judge)**: A distortion in the center that absorbs arguments.
- **Shockwaves**: Visual ripples when consensus shifts.

## Layout
- Organic and fluid.
- No rigid grids; floating, drifting elements.
- UI elements appear as runes or energy projections.

## Micro-Interactions
- Mouse movement influences particle flow.
- "Clashing" of elements produces sparks.
- Audio-visual sync (implied).
