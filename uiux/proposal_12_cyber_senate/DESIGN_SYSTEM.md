# Design System: Cyber Senate (Proposal 12)

## Philosophy
The "Cyber Senate" is a brutalist interpretation of AI governance. Here, logic is heavy, tangible, and unyielding. The aesthetic is industrial, combining raw data streams with massive, imposing geometric forms.

## Color Palette
- **Void Black**: `#000000`
- **Warning Red**: `#FF3333`
- **Tungsten Grey**: `#2A2A2A`
- **Data Amber**: `#FFAA00`
- **Terminal Green**: `#00FF00` (Accents)

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Chakra Petch', sans-serif (Square, technical)
- **Body**: 'Share Tech Mono', monospace (Terminal-like)

## Components
- **Senators**: Massive floating monoliths (cubes) representing AI nodes.
- **The Core**: A central pillar of light where consensus is fused.
- **Data Streams**: Lasers connecting senators to the core.

## Layout
- Rigid grid structure.
- Heavy use of borders and scanlines.
- UI elements feel like a military HUD.

## Micro-Interactions
- Glitch effects on hover.
- Mechanical sound cues (visualized).
- Sudden, sharp transitions.
