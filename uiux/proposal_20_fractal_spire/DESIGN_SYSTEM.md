# Design System: Fractal Spire (Proposal 20)

## Philosophy
"Fractal Spire" represents debate as the unfolding of complexity from simplicity. The "Judge" is a perfect, infinitely recursive geometric form. Robots are iterations of the fractal.

## Visual Metaphor
- **Debate**: Fractals expanding and changing shape.
- **Judge**: The Golden Ratio / Center of the Mandelbrot set.
- **Consensus**: Singular perfection in geometry.

## Color Palette
- **Void Purple**: `#240046`
- **Mystic Gold**: `#ff9e00`
- **Neon Pink**: `#f72585`
- **Cyber Blue**: `#4cc9f0`

## Typography
- **Headings**: 'Cinzel Decorative', serif (Ornamental)
- **Body**: 'Montserrat', sans-serif (Geometric)

## Interaction
- Zooming in endlessly.
- Kaleidoscopic effects.
- Elements rotate creating hypnotic patterns.
